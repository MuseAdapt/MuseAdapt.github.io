Prompt for Music Caption Generation

This page provides the exact prompt used for Gemini-2.5 Pro to generate music captions for our dataset construction pipeline. We present it here as supplementary rebuttal material.

Model
Gemini-2.5 Pro

Exact Prompt

Listen to the provided music and generate a detailed description strictly based on its content. Your description should be organized into the following sections:

1. **Instruments**: 
   - Identify the instruments used in the music.
   - Describe any notable features of their sound (e.g., texture, articulation, etc.).

2. **Timbre**:
   - Describe the **timbre** of each instrument used in the piece. 

3. **Genre and Style**:
   - Describe the genre or style of the music.
   - Include relevant characteristics (e.g., tempo, form, harmony, etc.).

4. **Emotion and Mood**:
   - Explain the emotions conveyed by the music.
   - Describe how the music achieves these emotions (e.g., through instrumentation, tempo, dynamics, etc.).

5. **Rhythm and Tempo**:
   - Note the rhythm and tempo of the piece.
   - Explain how the rhythm and tempo affect the overall feel of the music.

6. **Dynamics**:
   - Describe the variations in volume and intensity throughout the music.
   - Mention specific dynamics such as **crescendo**, where the volume gradually increases, and **decrescendo**, where the volume gradually decreases.
   - How do these changes in dynamics contribute to the emotional or structural development of the piece?

Answer in a paragraph. Ensure the description is concise yet thorough, based strictly on what is heard, and free of unrelated interpretations. The response should be in English with non-repetitive, varied sentences. Use clear, concise language, and make sure the description is well-organized and structured.

Usage Note

This prompt was designed to encourage captions that are descriptive, audio-grounded, and stylistically informative, while avoiding irrelevant interpretation. In particular, it asks Gemini-2.5 Pro to focus on instrumentation, timbre, genre/style, emotion, rhythm/tempo, and dynamics, and to return the final caption in a single concise paragraph.
